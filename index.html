About MemeDAO

MemeDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization for meme creators, enthusiasts, and collectors. Our mission is to empower creators and drive the meme economy forward through decentralized governance and collaboration.

How it Works

  1. Create and Submit Memes

    Members can create and submit their memes to the community for review and voting.

  2. Vote and Curate

    Community members vote on meme submissions, curating the best content and rewarding creators.

  3. Trade and Collect

    Buy, sell, and trade meme NFTs within the MemeDAO ecosystem, supporting the meme economy.

  4. Governance

    Participate in governance by voting on proposals and influencing the future of MemeDAO.
